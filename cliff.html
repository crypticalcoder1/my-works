Inducements

Inducement levels are levels in which retail traders;
  1. Are induced to buy in a bearish market
  2. Are induced to sell in an uptrend
  • Price tends to form bearish candles in an uptrend which are visible in lower tine frames signalling a false trend reversals
  • Price tends to form bullish candles in a downtrend in which are visible in lower time frames signalling a force trend reversals
  • In price action this inducements can be termed as retracements

fair value gaps

Fair value gaps are gaps which are caused by price imbalances
  • They signal imbalances between buyers and sellers.
  • In most scenarios price always comes to fill this imbalances.
  • They are characterized by low volumes.
  • In most cases low volumes are witnessed signalling lack of big players in the market.
  • Candles are very large witout wicks.
